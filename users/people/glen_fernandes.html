Glen Fernandes

Glen Fernandes is a C++ enthusiast who worked at Intel and, previously, Microsoft.

He lives with his wife, Caroline, and daughter, Aeryn, in Connecticut, in the US. He went to college in Sydney, Australia, and spent his childhood in Auckland, New Zealand.

He is a contributor to the Boost Smart Pointers and Core libraries, the author of the Boost Align library, and a contributor to several other Boost libraries.

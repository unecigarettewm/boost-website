Glen Fernandes

Glen Fernandes is a software engineer at Intel, and previously worked at Microsoft.

He lives with his wife, Caroline, in Seattle, Washington, in the US, but grew up in Sydney, Australia, after migrating from Auckland, New Zealand.

He is a contributor to the Boost Smart Pointers library, and the author of the Boost Align library.
